Our saunas at glance

Our saunas are not only a place of rest. If you integrate regular sessions into your everyday life, you also get benefits from periodical use.
When taking a sauna session, you should always make sure that you are at temperatures in which you feel comfortable with.

For your convenience, the spa will provide towels, bathrobes, slippers and all amenities required during your visit, of which you must make a deposit.
Bare in mind, Synthetic clothing (ex: Swimmwear) is not permited inside the sauna, but we request the use of towels as an overlay.
Shower is mandatory and we encourage you to do so, before and after a session.
